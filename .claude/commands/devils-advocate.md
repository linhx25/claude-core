  Challenges the current design, plan, or decision before you commit to it.
  Surfaces assumptions, risks, and alternatives. Use before making an
  irreversible choice or when a plan feels too comfortable.
---

## Devil's Advocate Workflow

1. **Identify what to challenge**
   - If a specific decision/plan was passed: challenge that
   - If not: ask "What decision or design should I challenge?"

2. **Challenge from three angles**

   **Assumption challenge**: What are we taking for granted that might be wrong?
   List the top 2 hidden assumptions in the current approach.

   **Risk challenge**: What's the most likely failure mode?
   Describe one concrete scenario where this approach fails badly.

   **Alternative challenge**: Is there a simpler or better approach?
   Propose one alternative worth considering (don't just validate the current plan).

3. **Present challenges** as direct statements, not hedged questions

4. **Ask**: "Given these, do you want to proceed, modify, or reconsider?"

5. **Do NOT recommend an answer** — present the challenges and let the user decide
